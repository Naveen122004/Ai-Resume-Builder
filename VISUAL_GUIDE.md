- **Top Bar**: Dark (#1a1a1a) with white text

- **Main Workspace**: White background
- **Build Panel**: Light gray (#f9fafb)
- **Footer**: Light gray (#f3f4f6)
- **Status Badge**: 
  - Not Started: Gray (#6b7280)
  - In Progress: Blue (#3b82f6)

## Interactive Elements

1. **Copy Button**: Copies artifact to clipboard
2. **Open Lovable**: Opens Lovable.dev in new tab
3. **It Worked**: Marks step complete, enables Next
4. **Error**: Visual feedback only
5. **File Upload**: Stores screenshot reference
6. **Previous/Next**: Navigate between steps
7. **View Proof**: Jump to proof page
8. **Step Cards**: Click to navigate to specific step
9. **Copy Final Submission**: Copies formatted submission text
